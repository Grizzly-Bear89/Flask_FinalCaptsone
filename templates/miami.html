Your Vacation to Magic City Starts Here!

Being the second largest city in Florida, Miami is known for having many cultural influences and a rich heritage. It is nicknamed the Capital of Latin America, mostly because of having a high population of Spanish-speakers. In fact, Miami is the second largest city in the U.S. with a dominant Spanish-speaking population. Miami culture has a focus around the performing arts, visual arts, cuisine, and dialect. Because of this vibrant culture, Miami has a second nickname known as the Magic City, due to the city being very lively overnight.

Miami Weather

The weather in Miami is known for being extremely hot in all times of the year, so be sure to pack light clothes and sunscreen if you plan on travelling there! Of course, the weather down there is at its hottest during the summer, so be sure to plan carefully when you plan to travel there. People tend to head to Miami during the summer, but the best time of year to head there is during the Spring. This is due to the weather not being influenced by the humidity of summer, along with hurricane season not starting at that time of year. It is the perfect weather environment to travel to Miami without having to worry about any of these factors in the way.
