Your Vacation to LA Starts Here!

Like many other major cities in the US, Los Angeles is also ethically diverse in the arts, cuisine, and cinema. The most notable one being cinema due to the presence of Hollywood. Hollywood is of course known as the home of the US film industry and many people visit Hollywood to see landmarks such as the Walk of Fame and the Chinese Theatre. Outside of cinema, Los Angeles has diversity in the arts due to the vast amount of architecture, visual arts, public arts, and art museums. There is definitely a lot of stuff to visit and do in Los Angeles, so feel free to pick and choose what to do when you visit there!

Los Angeles Weather

Los Angeles is known for having extremely humid and dry weather forecasts throughout the summer and cool and cloudy forecasts throughout the winter. In Los Angeles, the rainy season happens during winter, so if you are looking to head there without worrying about any sort of precipitation, it is better to travel there after winter.
